About Us

Welcome to Flybe Training Academy, an impressive and unique setting for your training and conferencing requirements in the heart of Devon. Our purpose built facility offers you an extensive choice of modern, bright and spacious meeting rooms in a variety of sizes and set-ups. Whether you require a large conference space for a formal event or an intimate space for your annual board meeting, we will work with you to accommodate your business needs and requirements.

We pride ourselves in the level of service we provide – our friendly experienced staff will assist you from the moment you make your initial enquiry through to the day of your event, ensuring your event runs as smoothly and successfully as possible.

Our location at Exeter International Airport, in the heart of Devon, means we are easily accessible by air, road or rail but just a stone’s throw away from miles of stunning coastline and beautiful countryside. Exeter is a vibrant city centre with its cathedral, modern shopping centre, continental cafés and stylish restaurants, making us the perfect place for your next meeting or event.

Reasons to choose us;
  • Choice of 26 meeting rooms including computer based training rooms
  • Large conference suite
  • Choice of quality catering options and on-site cafe
  • Large, free on-site car park
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Disabled access to all floors and induction loop available on request
  • On-site Hampton by Hilton Hotel with convenient accommodation for guests
