Hospitality

When using the conference facilities at Flybe Training Academy we have a range of bespoke hospitality packages to suit all types of meetings and events. Whether it’s for small groups of delegates or major events and conferences, our packages are designed specifically for your event!

With fresh, locally sourced, seasonal offerings we deliver high quality food with cold and hot options available.
