Conference suite & Meeting Rooms

Our spacious and practical Conference Suite can accommodate up to 130 delegates (theatre style) but can be split into two smaller rooms giving you the flexibility to use the additional space as a catering area, networking zone or break-out room. The Conference Suite comes equipped with free Wi-Fi, a PC, wide screen projection, integrated projector, wall mounted speakers and 2 linked plasma screens. Microphone and PA system available upon request.

We also have large and standard meeting rooms available to hire, equipped with AV Equipment and Sound Systems which can accommodate a range of different meetings and events.”
